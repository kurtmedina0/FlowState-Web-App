In Simple Terms:
It's essentially a SaaS platform similar to Notion, Asana, or Monday.com - a modern workspace tool that helps product teams collaborate, manage projects, automate workflows, and track progress all in one place.
The website serves as a landing page/marketing site to showcase the platform's features and convert visitors into customers through the pricing plans (Freelancer, Small Team, Business, Enterprise).
